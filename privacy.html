Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Overview

Best Shows ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you create a free member account, we collect:

  • Display name (chosen by you)
  • Email address
  • Show ratings you submit (1–10 scores per title)
  • Date of account creation

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or any information beyond what is required to operate your membership account.

Note: In the current version of Best Shows, all account data and ratings are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is transmitted to external servers. This means your data stays on your device.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate your membership session
  • Display your rating history on your personal dashboard
  • Calculate community average scores (aggregated, anonymous)

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

Best Shows does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. We do not run ads. Google Fonts is loaded from Google's servers — their privacy policy governs that interaction. We do not use Google Analytics or similar services.

Data Retention

Since data is stored locally in your browser, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or site data. We have no server-side records to delete.

Children's Privacy

Best Shows is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Significant changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@bestshows.com or use our Contact page.

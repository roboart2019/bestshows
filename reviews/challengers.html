Challengers

2024 • 131 min • Directed by Luca Guadagnino

A sexy, taut tennis drama that uses the sport as a metaphor for love, ambition, and power dynamics. Zendaya commands the screen as Tashi, a former tennis prodigy turned coach who manipulates the two men in her life. The film pulses with an erotic energy that's almost palpable.

Guadagnino's direction is precise and stylish. The editing by Marco Costa is a character in itself—split screens, rapid-fire cuts during matches, slow-motion close-ups that feel like intimate portraits. The score by Trent Reznor and Atticus Ross adds a throbbing, electronic undercurrent.

It's a film about competition in all its forms: on the court, in the bedroom, for status. The final match is edited to feel like a symphony. Not a traditional sports movie by any stretch—more like a psychological thriller disguised as a romance. Recommended if you like intense, stylish dramas with a bite.

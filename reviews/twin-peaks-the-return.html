Twin Peaks: The Return

2017 · Showtime / Paramount+ · Limited Series (18 Parts)

Twin Peaks: The Return is not television. It is an 18-hour film by David Lynch — a director who operates by his own logic — and it should be approached as such.

Kyle MacLachlan plays multiple versions of Dale Cooper across timelines that collapse into each other. The first four parts are deliberately disorienting. Parts 8 and 17-18 are among the most extraordinary things ever produced for any screen.

The Return is an elegy — for the original series, for the 1990s, for the promise that heroes can fix what's broken. Cooper is trapped, diminished, and ultimately unable to save anyone. The final image is not hope. The show earned the right to that ending through 17 hours of magnificent, maddening commitment to its own vision. Lynch at his most uncompromising.

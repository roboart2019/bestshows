Atlanta

2016–2022 · FX / Hulu · 4 Seasons

Atlanta is unlike anything else on television. Created by and starring Donald Glover, it begins as a music industry comedy and gradually becomes something stranger, funnier, and more unnerving.

Glover's Earn is a Princeton dropout managing his rapper cousin Paper Boi (Brian Tyree Henry, magnificent). Lakeith Stanfield's Darius is the show's id — a philosophical eccentric who exists slightly outside reality. Together they navigate Atlanta's music scene, but the show uses that frame to examine race, poverty, and the surreal texture of American life.

Season 2 ("Robbin' Season") is the peak: a series of near-horror vignettes that build into a sustained nightmare. Episodes like "Teddy Perkins" and "Barbershop" are standalone masterpieces. Atlanta is genuinely unlike anything else. That's rare.

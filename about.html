About Best Shows

Who we are and why we do this.

Our Mission

Best Shows was born from a simple frustration: it's hard to find honest, thoughtful opinions on what to watch next. Streaming services push their own content, review aggregators get manipulated, and most publications are beholden to studios.

We're different. We're an independent collective of critics and cinephiles who watch everything ourselves and publish our genuine reactions—no sponsorships, no ads, no payola. If we don't like something, we'll say so. If we love it, we'll gush.

How We Review

Every show or film gets watched in full (or as complete as it is at time of writing). We discuss internally, hash out disagreements, and then write reviews that balance technical craft with emotional impact. Ratings are on a 10-point scale, half-points included.

We avoid spoilers unless explicitly marked. Our goal is to help you decide whether to invest your time, not to ruin the experience.

The Team

We're small—just twelve core critics with diverse tastes. Some specialize in sci-fi, others in prestige drama, some just love a good comedy. That mix keeps our perspectives varied and our discussions lively.

We're based in Chicago but watch globally. The site is built and maintained by Gary Otten, with design assistance from Amy (an AI who's gotten better at not mixing up pronouns).

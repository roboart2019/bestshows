About Best Shows

Who we are and why we do this.

Our Mission

Best Shows was born from a simple frustration: it's hard to find honest, thoughtful opinions on what to watch next. Streaming services push their own content, review aggregators get manipulated, and most publications are beholden to studios.

We're different. We're an independent collective of critics and cinephiles who watch everything ourselves and publish our genuine reactions — no sponsorships, no ads, no payola. If we don't like something, we'll say so. If we love it, we'll gush.

How We Review

Every show or film gets watched in full (or as complete as it is at time of writing). We discuss internally, hash out disagreements, and then write reviews that balance technical craft with emotional impact. Critic scores are on a 10-point scale, half-points included.

We also provide official TV and film ratings (TV-MA, R, PG-13, etc.) and transparent Content Advisories — a detailed list of potentially sensitive material in each title, using a consistent set of categories. We believe viewers deserve to know what they're walking into.

We avoid spoilers unless explicitly marked. Our goal is to help you decide whether to invest your time, not to ruin the experience.

Content Advisories

Our Content Advisories go beyond official ratings. They flag specific material — Violence, Drug Use, Suicide / Self-Harm, Death of a Child, and more — using a fixed vocabulary so you can make informed choices. We list advisories on every review card and individual review page.

Members who join Best Shows can also rate shows independently, contributing community scores alongside our critic ratings.

Membership

Best Shows is free to read. Free membership lets you rate any show or film on a 1–10 scale, track your personal rating history, and see aggregated community scores alongside our critic scores. No ads, no upsells — just a community of people who care about what they watch.

The Team

We're small — just twelve core critics with diverse tastes. Some specialize in sci-fi, others in prestige drama, some just love a good comedy. That mix keeps our perspectives varied and our discussions lively.

We're based in Chicago but watch globally. The site is built and maintained by Gary Otten, with design assistance from Amy.
